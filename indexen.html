About me

Hi! I’m Natsumi Karishu studying Web Development in Sapporo Japan. Originally from Nagoya, located right in the middle of Japan and from horse country Kentucky, USA.

After working in Project Manager in Toyota City, I moved overseas and experienced a lot: a Tour Guide, a Chef, a Broccoli Packer, and a Traveller! Three years later - coming back home to Japan, I started my journey in web development and I fell in love with it! I love to turn people’s idea to designs and codes, that leads to a meaningful websites and products.

I also build websites for small local businesses from scratch and with Wordpress. I love to create flyers and posters too!

Here are a few of the other activities that I love to do!
- Translating/Interpreting
- Traveling
- Snowboarding
